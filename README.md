# MallowMals — Cerealsona Personality Quiz

An interactive personality quiz disguised as a blind box unboxing experience. Instead of it being randomly selected, the character you receive is a reflection of **your personality and choices**.

## Concept

The experience unfolds through a chatbot conversation on a simulated phone screen. A mysterious message from an unknown number invites you to take a quiz and depending on how you respond, you're guided through 12 questions about your day.

Your choices map to one of six characters, each representing a different personality type. After the quiz, you shake a blind box to build anticipation before your character is revealed.
